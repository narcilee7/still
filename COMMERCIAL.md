# Still Commercial & Open Source Policy

This repository (`narcilee7/still`) is the **open-source Community Edition** of Still.

unless otherwise noted in a file header.

## What is open source

You are free to:

- Use, modify, and self-host the Community Edition for personal or internal use.
- Build plugins, themes, and third-party clients on top of the public API.
- Contribute improvements back to the project (see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)).

## What is not open source

The following are provided only through the official Still service or a commercial
license:

- **Managed hosting / official SaaS**: the hosted app available at `https://still.app`
  (placeholder) and the official mobile apps distributed through app stores.
- **Premium AI features**: advanced mood interpretation models, personalized
  recommendation algorithms, and proprietary prompt strategies.
- **Enterprise features**: admin dashboards, moderation tooling, analytics,
  billing, SLA-backed support, and custom deployments.
- **Official branding**: the "Still" name, logo, and app icons are trademarks of
  NarciLee and may not be used without permission.

These components live in a separate, private repository and are not covered by
this open-source license.

## Why this split

Still is an AI-native consumer product. Open-sourcing the core lets the community
audit, self-host, and contribute, while the official service funds ongoing
development of models, infrastructure, and design.
